How to Contribute

There are many ways you can support Unity Church on the Mountain: with your love and prayers; your presence at Sunday services and classes; and your financial support, too.

PayPal

Dontate directly through PayPal. You can make one time donations or set-up recurring monthly donations.

Daily Good

Good Search offers a portion of their profits to non-profit organizations. Any time you shop online through Daily Good (Good Search) and designate Unity Church On The Mountain as the recipient, you are giving back to our Spiritual Community! Click here to learn more.

Amazon Smile

Amazon will donate 0.5% of the price of your eligible AmazonSmile purchases to Unity Church On the Mountain whenever you shop on AmazonSmile. AmazonSmile is the same Amazon you know. Same products, same prices, same service. Support your charitable organization by starting your shopping at smile.amazon.com!

Kroger

Kroger Community Rewards makes fund-raising easy..all you have to do is shop at Kroger and swipe your Plus Card! Click here to enroll and learn more.
