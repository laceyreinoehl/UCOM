Our Location

1328 Governors Dr. SE Huntsville, AL 35801

Located on beautiful Monte Sano Mountain in the heart of Huntsville, AL Unity Church on the Mountain is situated in a spot that feels secluded and private, surrounded by trees you immediately feel relaxed and serene.

From I-565 Eastbound (Madison / Decatur)

  • Head northeast on I-565 E
  • Take exit 19A to merge onto US-231 S/US-431 S/Memorial Pkwy SW toward Memorial Pkwy/li>
  • Take the exit toward US-431 S
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Governors Dr SW
  • Continue on Governors Drive… go across California Street…Unity’s driveway is 4/10 of a mile on the right

From I-565 Westbound (Gurley / Scottsboro

  • Driving West on Hwy/Alt 72
  • Continue onto I-565 W
  • Take exit 20 from I-565 W
  • Continue onto Andrew Jackson Way NE / SE California St to Governors Drive SE
  • Turn left onto Governors Drive…Unity’s driveway is 4/10 of a mile on the right
