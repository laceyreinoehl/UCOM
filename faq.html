FAQ's for Newcomers

What time are your Sunday services?

Discussion Class begins at 9:30am Sunday Service begins at 11:00am

How long are your services?

Sunday services typically last about one (1) hour.

What do I wear?

Please dress however you are most comfortable. From blue jeans to your Sunday bes, we welcome you no matter your attire.

I have children; do you have activities for them during services?

We have Sunday school available upstairs during our 11am service for children ages 4 - 10

Is Unity accepting of other denominations?

Unity Church on the Mountain is an all inclusive spiritual community. We welcome people of all faiths, denominations, and lifestyles.

How do I get involved?

Find one of our board members at the next Sunday service or email unitychurchonthemountain@gmail.com to speak with someone about how you would like to be more involved in Unity Church on the Mountain

What resources are available at Unity Church on the Mountain?

We have a small bookstore located upstairs with a variety of books and resources for spiritual inspiration and to help you learn more about Unity
